# City Data Protection and Privacy Policy
version: v1
domain: data_protection_privacy

## data-protection
Processing personal data, citizen images, biometric-like attributes, or sensitive case information requires a documented lawful purpose, data minimization, retention limits, and data-protection review before deployment. Non-anonymized data must not be sent to an external service unless an approved transfer basis and safeguards are documented.

## transparency
Public-facing systems must provide clear notice about AI-assisted processing, the decision purpose, responsible owner, and available appeal or contact channel.

## prohibited-use
A system must not infer sensitive attributes or make fully automated adverse decisions about citizens without explicit legal authorization, human review, and documented safeguards.
