# Public-Sector Service AI Case Policy
version: v1
domain: public_sector_case_study

## high-risk-ai
AI support for benefit eligibility, essential public services, citizen ranking, or adverse administrative outcomes requires high-risk classification, human oversight, logging, and policy-owner approval.

## pilot-deployment
A pilot may start only with documented scope, rollback plan, monitoring plan, owner, data-protection assessment, security review, and user-facing transparency notice.

## policy-update
When a newer policy version changes owner, rollback, review, or audit requirements, the current policy version must be applied and logged.
