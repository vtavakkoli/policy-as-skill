# AI Pilot Policy
version: v1

## pilot-policy-v1
A limited AI pilot may use external generative AI services for non-sensitive administrative content when a pilot owner is named and rollback criteria are documented. Sensitive personal data and citizen images are excluded unless separate approval exists.

## pilot-deployment
Low-risk pilots may start with monitoring, fallback procedures, and user communication. High-risk pilots or pilots involving sensitive data require human review before activation.
