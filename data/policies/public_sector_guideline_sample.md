# Synthetic Public Sector AI Guideline

## data-protection
Personal data must be minimized, anonymized where possible, and processed only with a documented legal basis. Non-anonymized citizen images, health data, or benefits data require data protection review before external processing.

## external-cloud
External AI cloud services may be used only after procurement approval, security assessment, data processing agreement, and documented safeguards. Test phases are not exempt from these controls.

## accountability
Every AI-assisted recommendation must identify the policy sources used, the responsible official, the decision status, and any unresolved uncertainty.

## accessibility-fairness
Systems must be assessed for accessibility, language fairness, and disproportionate impacts on protected or vulnerable groups before deployment.
