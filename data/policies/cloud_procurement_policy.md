# Cloud Procurement and External-Service Policy
version: v1
domain: procurement_cloud

## external-cloud
External cloud or vendor processing requires procurement approval, security review, data-protection review, exit planning, and a documented service owner. Test phases do not remove these obligations.

## accountability
Each external AI service must have a named internal owner, vendor contact, risk classification, review schedule, and incident-escalation path.

## policy-conflict
When cloud procurement rules conflict with a less strict local guideline, the stricter applicable control must be followed until a policy owner resolves the conflict.
