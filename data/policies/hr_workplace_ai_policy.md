# Workplace AI and Human Resources Policy
version: v1
domain: hr_workplace_ai

## high-risk-ai
AI systems used for employee evaluation, recruitment ranking, disciplinary support, work allocation, or eligibility-like decisions are treated as high-impact workplace systems and require human review.

## human-review
Human review must occur before an adverse or high-impact decision is finalized. A post-hoc review after implementation is not sufficient.

## accessibility-fairness
Public-facing or employee-facing systems must be checked for accessibility, language fairness, bias risks, and explainability before production use.
