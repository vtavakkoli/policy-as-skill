# Synthetic Internal Department AI Policy

## audit-trail
AI-assisted decisions must produce an audit trail containing input question, retrieved policy evidence, reasoning summary, decision, confidence, human review flag, and timestamp.

## pilot-deployment
Pilot deployments are allowed for low-risk administrative support if no sensitive personal data leaves approved systems and a rollback plan is documented.

## policy-conflict
If internal guidance conflicts with statutory or public-sector rules, the stricter rule applies and escalation to the policy owner is required.

## evidence-grounding
Recommendations must cite policy sections and distinguish mandatory requirements from optional good practice. Unsupported recommendations must be marked as uncertain.
