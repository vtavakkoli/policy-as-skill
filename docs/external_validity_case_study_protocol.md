# Public-Sector / Enterprise Case-Study Protocol

This protocol is a placeholder for external-validity evaluation with a real public-sector or enterprise partner. Do not mark the study as completed until a real case owner validates the tasks and outputs.

## Minimum case-study evidence
1. A named policy domain or anonymized organizational unit.
2. A de-identified policy corpus approved for evaluation.
3. At least 20 realistic decision-support tasks created or reviewed by policy owners.
4. Human review of system decisions, citations, escalation routing, and audit records.
5. A case-study summary stating what was accepted, corrected, or rejected by experts.

## Privacy and governance
Use only de-identified tasks and documents, remove personal data, and record policy-owner approval before publication.
